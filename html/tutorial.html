Admin Tutorial

  • Step 1: Login

    • Go to the top left corner and select "admin login".
    • Enter in credentials and select the organization.
    • Hit the login button.

  • Step 2: Choose Organization

    • Once logged in, choose the link of your organization.

  • Step 3: Choose the style

    • If you want kata, you will already be in kata after you hit the link.
    • If you want kumite, hit the kumite button that is on the right of the page.

  • Step 4: Kata

    • Select the number of participants.
    • Then hit "Create Table".
    • A table should appear. Something like what is below.
    • Enter the ID in the ID column to bring up registered players.
    • If the person is not registered, type their name in the name column.
    • Now, start entering in the scores of the judges. It should automatically add up the scores on the right.
    • Once done hit "Find Winners"
    • The names will be displayed in a table that looks like what is below.
    • Finally hit add to season to add the points to the winners for that season.

  • Step 5: Kumite

    • Select the number of participants.
    • Then hit "GO".
    • A table should appear. Something like what is below.
    • Enter the ID in the ID column to bring up registered players.
    • If the person is not registered, type their name in the name column.
    • Next, hit "Create Bracket".
    • A fight box and a bracket with the names entered, should appear.
    • Enter the score of each person, and hit score.
    • This will proceed the winner and you will keep on doing this until they reach the last part of the bracket.
    • Depending on how many players are entered, the fight might be short.
    • A loser bracket will appear after the first major bracket is done. It will appear under the main bracket
    • Then the loser fight box will show. Do the same as beore.
    • A fight for fourth box might appear if you have enough players.
    • After all is done, the winners will be placed in the bottom tables of the webpage.
    • Finally hit add to season to add the points to the winners for that season.

  • Step 6: Signout

    • Go up to the top left corner and hit "Signout".
    • You should now be logged out.
